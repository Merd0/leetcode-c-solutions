## Study Rule

1. Write and explain your own approach before comparing alternatives.
2. Preserve a useful brute-force attempt when it teaches why the optimized method is needed.
3. For heap allocations, state ownership and cleanup before moving on.
4. Continue with a [focused practice track](PRACTICE_TRACKS.md) after completing the roadmap.
